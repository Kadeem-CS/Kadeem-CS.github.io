AI & the Stock Market

Artificial Intelligence is transforming how stocks are analyzed, traded, and managed by financial institutions and individual investors.

What is AI in Stock Trading?

AI in stock trading refers to the use of machine learning algorithms and data analysis techniques to predict market trends and automate trading decisions.

How AI Is Used

  • Algorithmic trading
  • Market trend prediction
  • Risk management
  • Portfolio optimization

Ethical Concerns

AI trading raises concerns about fairness, transparency, market manipulation, and unequal access to advanced technology.
